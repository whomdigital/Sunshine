Sunshine Detailing

Welcome to the ultimate boat & yacht detailing service in South Florida.

Sunshine Detailing is a leader in Miami Beach, providing superior dockside services since 2004. The company was created by the owner learning the trade from the basics and establishing processes that our team of experts use consistently on every job. He is still today on top of our daily operations to bring innovation and guarantee the best customer experience.

Having provided high-quality services for more than 15 years, we’ve done literally hundreds of boats of every type and brand in order to know immediately what your boat needs. Still, what really sets us apart from our competitors is the passion we put on every project. Highly demanding boat owners, captains, brokers, manufacturers, and even marinas that give us in-house exclusivity, have trusted their vessels to us, as fully satisfied customers.

Search no more, explore our website and give us a call to start enjoying a hassle-free boat maintenance experience with the best boat detailing company from Las Olas to Gables by the Sea.
