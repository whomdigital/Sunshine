ABOUT US

Summary

Sunshine Detailing a Licensed and Insured marine detailing company based in Miami Beach since 2004. We are mobile service covering from Palm Beach to The Keys and we have developed a work methodology that gives 100% peace of mind to local and international clients.

We only use the best practices and leading products, tools and process in the market for guaranteed results and durability on each job. More than 15 years in business provides us with the pride and certainty that no other detailing team will do a better job than us for the price.

Our status reports with pictures of improvements or issues we find on your vessel can be reviewed in real time from the comfort of your phone or computer. We surround ourselves with a carefully built network of vendors, so instead of having to try luck or research about a canvas guy, mechanic or fiberglass repair, just give us a call and we’ll provide you with the information of proven vendors.

To view some of our past projects click on our gallery . If you are looking for a fully reliable and expert service to enjoy hassle-free boating, contact us today! "SunShine" your vessel with Sunshine Detailing!
