ABOUT US

Welcome to the ultimate boat & yacht detailing service in South Florida since 2004

Sunshine Detailing is a Licensed and Insured marine detailing company based in Miami Beach since 2004. We are a mobile service covering from Palm Beach to the Keys and we have developed a work methodology that gives 100% peace of mind to both local and international clients.

We only use the best practices and leading products, tools and process in the market for guaranteed results and durability on each job. More than 15 years in business provides us with the pride and certainty that no other detailing team will do a better job than us for the price.

Our status reports with before and after pictures or any issues we find on your vessel can be reviewed in real time form your mobile to take action inmediately as we see them. We also surround ourselves with a carefully built network of vendors, so instead of having to try luck or research about a canvas guy, mechanic or fiberglass repair, our clients know that are taken care through a network of proven partners on all their maintenance needs.

To view some of our past projects click on our GALLERY . If you are looking for a fully reliable and expert service to enjoy hassle-free boat maintenance, search no more and CONTACT us today for a free quote and a custom plan!

Monthly Maintenance Plans
